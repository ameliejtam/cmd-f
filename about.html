About GreenHeap

Climate change impacts everything from weather patterns and food security to economic stability and public health. Understanding the latest scientific research, policies, and events helps us make informed decisions about sustainability, energy use, and environmental responsibility, helping us prepare, adapt, and advocate for climate solutions. Being consistently updated in the climate justice world can be challenging. With GreenHeap, we aim to provide weekly, summarized sources of current events and changes. Knowledge empowers action, and staying knowledgable is key to building a resilient and sustainable future.
